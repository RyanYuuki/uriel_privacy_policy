PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 10/12/2025

Uriel (“the App”) is a personal library application that allows users to save, organize, and track anime, movie, and series entries. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information We Collect
The App may collect the following user-provided information:
• Profile name
• Profile picture (selected from device storage)
• User-created entries and progress information
This data is stored locally on your device unless exported manually.

Automatically Collected Data
The App uses Firebase services, which may collect non-personal device and usage data, including:
• Device type
• App interactions
• Crash information
• Analytics events
This information helps improve app stability and performance.

Permissions Used
• READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Used for selecting a profile picture and handling user-created export/backup data. The App does not access unrelated files.
• INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required for Firebase Analytics, metadata fetching, and network operations.

How We Use Information
Information is used to provide library functionality, personalization, and app performance improvements. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Data Storage and Security
• Personal data remains stored locally on your device.
• Analytics and crash logs stored by Firebase are handled according to Google’s Firebase Privacy and Security policies.

Children’s Privacy
The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain inappropriate content.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Firebase may collect anonymized usage data, which contains no personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services
The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may collect non-identifiable data related to usage and device performance.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App implies acceptance of the updated policy.
