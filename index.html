Introduction

Uriel ("the App") is a personal library application that allows users to save, organize, and track anime, movie, and series entries. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information We Collect

The App may collect the following user-provided information:

  • Profile name
  • Profile picture (selected from device storage)
  • User-created entries and progress information

This data is stored locally on your device unless exported manually.

Automatically Collected Data

The App uses Firebase services, which may collect non-personal device and usage data, including:

  • Device type
  • App interactions
  • Crash information
  • Analytics events

This information helps improve app stability and performance.

Permissions Used

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
INTERNET
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Storage permissions: Used for selecting a profile picture and handling user-created export/backup data. The App does not access unrelated files.

Internet/network permissions: Required for Firebase Analytics, metadata fetching, and network operations.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide library functionality, personalization, and app performance improvements. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties except anonymized analytics sent to Firebase as described below.

Data Storage and Security

Personal data remains stored locally on your device. Analytics and crash logs stored by Firebase are handled according to Google's Firebase Privacy and Security policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain inappropriate content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under applicable age thresholds. Firebase may collect anonymized usage data, which contains no personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may collect non-identifiable data related to usage and device performance. Review Firebase's privacy documentation for details.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App implies acceptance of the updated policy.
